UPSC Civil Services Result 2022 out, Shruti Sharma tops exam, top 3 are women

This time, Shruti Sharma has emerged as topper. Ankita Agarwal came second and Gamini Singla is the third topper.

UPSC Topper 2021: Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) on May 30 announced final result of the Civil Services Examination, 2021. This time, Shruti Sharma has emerged as topper. Ankita Agarwal came second and Gamini Singla is the third topper. The first three toppers are women candidates. The final result has been published after prelims, main and interview rounds. As many as 685 candidates have qualified the prestigious test and of them, 244 are from general category, 73 from Economically Weaker Sections , 203 of Other Backward Classes, 105 Scheduled Caste and 60 from the Scheduled Tribes. Congratulating the successful candidates, Prime Minister Narendra Modi tweeted: Congratulations to all those who have cleared the Civil Services (Main) Examination, 2021.
